About Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories worldwide. It also sells various related services. The company's iPhone line includes the iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 15 Pro Max, iPhone 15, iPhone 15 Plus, iPhone 14, iPhone 14 Plus, iPhone 13 mini, iPhone 13, iPhone 12, and iPhone SE. Its Mac line includes the MacBook Air and MacBook Pro laptops, and the iMac, Mac Pro, Mac Studio, and Mac mini desktop computers.
